Safety Tips

Your safety is important. Please follow these tips while using Talk and Chat.

Do Not Share Personal Details

Do not share your phone number, home address, school/college details, passwords, bank details, or private information.

Use Report and Leave

If someone behaves badly, use the Report button. You can also use Next or Leave anytime.

Be Respectful

Treat other users with respect. Do not harass, threaten, abuse, or spam others.

Be Careful With Strangers

Remember that strangers online may not always be honest. Avoid trusting unknown people with personal matters.

Audio Call Safety

Use audio calls carefully. You can mute your microphone or leave the call anytime.
