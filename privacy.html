Privacy Policy

Talk and Chat respects your privacy. This page explains how our website handles user information.

Information We Collect

We do not ask users to create an account. We do not ask for your name, email, or password to start chatting.

Chat Messages

Text chat messages are used only to connect users in real time. We do not display your identity to strangers.

Reports

If a user reports another user, basic report information may be saved for safety and moderation purposes.

Audio Calls

Audio calls use browser microphone permission. You can allow or deny microphone access from your browser settings.

Personal Safety

Do not share personal information such as phone number, address, passwords, bank details, or private photos with strangers.
